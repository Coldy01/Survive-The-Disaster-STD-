THE GROUND BEGINS TO SHAKE

An earthquake is happening! Remember to stay calm

and do the Duck, cover, and hold!

Level Complete!

DUCK

Lower your body to the ground quickly and safely. This position protects you from falling objects and debris during an earthquake.

COVER

Take cover under a sturdy desk or table. If that's not available, cover your head and neck with your arms and crouch in an inside corner of the building.

HOLD

Hold on to your shelter (desk or table) with one hand and be prepared to move with it. Stay in this position until the shaking stops completely.

Remember: Duck, Cover, Hold!

Debris Fall!

Debris fell to your head and you died! Always remember to do the duck, cover and hold when the ground shakes.

You must press "E" to duck, cover, and hold! immediately!
