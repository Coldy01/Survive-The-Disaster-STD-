Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start the game?

Click the "Start" button on the main menu to begin your adventure.

What are the different disasters?

The game features earthquake and fire disasters. Each has unique challenges and strategies.

What is the goal of our website?

Our goal with our website is to give awereness to grade 11 STI student about what they have to do in emergency situations.

Can I adjust the volume?

Yes, use the volume slider in the Settings menu to control the background music.

What does fullscreen mode do?

Fullscreen mode expands the game to fill your entire screen for a more immersive experience.

How do I mute the audio?

Click the "🔊 Mute" button in the Settings menu to toggle audio on and off.
