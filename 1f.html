ALERT

Immediately activate the fire alarm when you see fire or smoke. Shout "Fire!" to alert everyone nearby and help others become aware of the danger.

EVACUATE

Leave the building immediately using the nearest safe exit. Walk quickly but don't run. Stay low if there's smoke. Never use elevators during a fire.

ASSEMBLE

Once outside, go to the designated assembly point. Stay there and wait for further instructions from emergency personnel. Do not re-enter the building until it is declared safe.
